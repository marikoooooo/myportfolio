Somatic Design Project

Creating two innovative products that provide users with meaningful somatic experiences through design and technology

Arduino IDE Apple Freeform Product Design Prototyping

Project Overview

This somatic design project explores how technology can create meaningful physical experiences that connect with our bodies and daily rhythms. I designed two complementary products that work together to improve sleep and wake cycles through gentle, body-aware interactions.

The first product is a breathing sleep lamp that uses soft blue light to create a calming "breathing" pattern, helping users relax into sleep. The second is a vibrating wake-up bracelet with a companion app that gently wakes users without the jarring experience of traditional alarms, creating a more natural transition from sleep to wakefulness.

Design Time

6-8 weeks

Team Size

4 people

Project Type

School Assignment

Product Features

Breathing Sleep Lamp

Handcrafted cardboard housing with Arduino Uno and Elegoo components creating soft blue light that gently "breathes" in a rhythm designed to help users naturally fall asleep through synchronized breathing.

Vibrating Wake-Up Bracelet

Crocheted wearable design integrated with Arduino Uno and Elegoo vibration components that create gentle patterns gradually increasing to wake users naturally without harsh interruption.

Companion Mobile App

Interactive app prototype that allows users to customize wake-up settings and monitor their sleep patterns.

Somatic Integration

Both products work together to create a holistic sleep experience that respects the body's natural rhythms and responses.

Design Process

This project required deep research into somatic design principles and understanding how physical products can create meaningful bodily experiences. The design process focused on creating gentle, non-intrusive interactions that work with natural body rhythms.

Tools & Methods

  • Hardware: Arduino Uno, Elegoo Fun Kit for electronics components
  • Materials: Cardboard construction for lamp housing, crochet yarn for bracelet
  • Programming: Arduino IDE for programming lamp breathing patterns and bracelet vibration
  • Design: Apple Freeform for conceptual design and user flow mapping
  • Prototyping: Physical product sketches and digital app mockups
  • Research: Sleep cycle research and somatic experience studies

Design Philosophy

The core philosophy centered on creating technology that supports rather than disrupts natural body processes. Both products were designed to be subtle, gentle, and responsive to individual user needs and preferences.

Design Principles

Core Somatic Design Principles:
• Gentle Interaction: Technology that whispers, not shouts
• Body Awareness: Designs that respond to natural rhythms
• Gradual Transition: Smooth movement between sleep and wake states
• Sensory Harmony: Light, touch, and vibration working together
• Personal Adaptation: Customizable to individual preferences
• Minimal Disruption: Supporting rather than interrupting natural processes

What I Learned

Somatic Design Thinking

Gained understanding of how to design technology that works with the body rather than against it, creating meaningful physical experiences.

Arduino Programming

Developed skills in programming microcontrollers to create responsive, pattern-based lighting that mimics natural breathing rhythms.

Holistic Product Design

Learned to design complementary products that work together as a system, considering the entire user experience from sleep to wake.

Future Improvements

If I were to continue developing these somatic design products, I would focus on:

  • Integration with sleep tracking sensors for more personalized experiences
  • Machine learning algorithms to adapt to individual sleep patterns over time
  • Additional sensory elements like gentle sounds or aromatherapy integration
  • Smart home connectivity for coordinated environmental adjustments
  • Clinical testing to validate the effectiveness of somatic sleep interventions
  • Development of a full product line for comprehensive sleep wellness
