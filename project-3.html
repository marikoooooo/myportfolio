UXstify

A social media-inspired job matching app connecting UX designers with career opportunities

Google Slides UI/UX Design Prototyping Social Media Design

Project Overview

UXstify is an innovative app prototype that revolutionizes how UX designers connect with job opportunities by combining social media functionality with job search capabilities. The app creates a platform where UX designers can showcase their work, network with peers, and discover career opportunities in an engaging, social environment.

This project focused on creating a user-centered design that would help UX professionals build their personal brand, connect with like-minded designers, and find job opportunities that match their skills and interests through an intuitive, social media-inspired interface.

Development Time

3-4 weeks

Team Size

6 people

Project Type

School Project

Key Features

Social Profile Building

Create compelling profiles showcasing UX design portfolios, skills, and career achievements in a social media format.

Job Matching Algorithm

Intelligent matching system that connects UX designers with job opportunities based on skills, experience, and preferences.

Networking Features

Social networking capabilities allowing designers to connect, share work, and engage with the UX community.

Career Discovery

Explore job opportunities, company cultures, and career paths through an engaging, social media-inspired interface.

Design Process

The UXstify prototype was developed using a user-centered design approach, focusing on the specific needs and pain points of UX designers in their job search and career networking.

Tools & Methods

  • Prototyping: Google Slides for interactive prototype creation
  • Design: UI/UX design principles for social media interfaces
  • Methodology: Design Thinking Process and user research
  • Research: Interviews with UX majors to understand their needs for a hypothetical job matching app

Design Challenges & Solutions

The main challenge was creating an interface that combined the engaging aspects of social media with the functional requirements of job search platforms. We solved this by implementing familiar social media patterns while integrating job matching and career discovery features seamlessly.

Design Principles

Key Design Principles:
• Social Engagement: Familiar social media patterns and interactions
• Career Focus: Job search functionality integrated seamlessly
• Personal Branding: Showcase design portfolios and achievements
• Community Building: Foster connections within the UX community
• Intuitive Matching: Easy-to-use job matching and discovery features
